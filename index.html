About us.

Our Mission
We have one vision,to show the world that Disability is not Inability,our goal is to ensure that we help all our esteemed members reach their maximum potential and to live with fellow people and live a normal life.We also focus on fitness and happiness of our members with frequent sport events and also pshycological sessions every end of month.
Our History.
The Zedi foundation was founded by Abdullah Tayeb in 2023.He always had an idea that disability doesn't limit anyone to being the best versions of themselves,he started it as a small organisation a few years back and he is now an exiting prospect in the Digital Business World.
